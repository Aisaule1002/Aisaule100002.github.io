📝 English Practice Tasks Using New Words

  1. Fill in the blanks: Complete the sentences with the correct word (Dream, Hope, Believe, Heart, Strong).
    • 1. Never stop ________ in yourself.
    • 2. Follow your ________ wherever it goes.
    • 3. Stay ________ even when life is hard.
    • 4. Everyone has a ________ to make the world better.
    • 5. My biggest ________ is to speak English fluently.
  2. Translate: Write the Kazakh translation of each new word.
  3. Make sentences: Write your own 5 English sentences using the new words.
  4. Matching: Match the English words with their Kazakh meanings.
    • a. Dream → _____
    • b. Hope → _____
    • c. Believe → _____
    • d. Heart → _____
    • e. Strong → _____
  5. Creative task: Write a short poem or 3–4 lines using all the new words together.

💬 Example: I believe in my dream, my heart is strong, and I never lose hope.
